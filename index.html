Committed to safety in the EVSE industry

Experts in Safety

WMK Safety Solutions is here to equip you with the safety knowledge pertaining to electric vehicle safety equipment (EVSE), or more commonly referred to as 'charging stations'.

Whether you are a first responder or a new owner of EVSE, it's vital to equip yourself with the know-how to keep you and your surroundings safe.

The purpose of this website is to be a reliable resource for both EVSE safety and up-to-date news pertaining to everything from industry regulations to infrastructure-related activities.
